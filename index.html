without spending all your money

... but not just any chocolate chip cookie

I have dedicated this entire semester to trying and developing the best chocolate chip cookies you will ever eat... in a kitchen with zero baking tools.

If I were in my own kitchen with all of the proper tools and ingredients, this wouldn't have taken this many tries. If you've ever tried to cook - or worse... bake - in someone else's kitchen (someone who doesn't bake), you probably feel my pain.

Dealing with substitutes when I'm trying to develop a brand new recipe could be great, but not this time. I'm in a someone else's kitchen, a completely new state, and pretty much zero baking tools - by baking tools... I mean... literally, a whisk.
